AVID Strategies

W.I.C.O.R

The strategies listed below offer a variety of different methods to use in the classroom. These strategies help to prepare students for their future. These strategies are incorporated into daily lessons without many teachers even realizing it.

    AVID Strategies

    • Cornell Notes, Quick Writes, Reflections, Process Writing, Peer Evaluating, Learning Logs, Journals

      • Inquiry:

    • Costa's Level of Questioning, Skilled Questioning, Socratic Seminars, Quick Write, Discussion, Investigation, Tutorials

      • Collaboration:

    • Costa's Level of Questioning, Tutorials, Jigsaw Activities, Peer Editing Group, Philisophical Chairs, Service Learning Projects

      • Organization:
