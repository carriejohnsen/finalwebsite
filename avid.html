AVID Strategies

W.I.C.O.R

The strategies listed below offer a variety of different methods to use in the classroom. These strategies help to prepare students for their future.

    AVID Strategies

    W.I.C.O.R

    • Writing: Cornell Notes, Quick Writes, Reflections, Process Writing, Peer Evaluating, Learning Logs, Journals
    • Inquiry: Costa's Level of Questioning, Skilled Questioning, Socratic Seminars, Quick Write, Discussion, Investigation, Tutorials
    • Collaboration: Costa's Level of Questioning, Tutorials, Jigsaw Activities, Peer Editing Group, Philisophical Chairs, Service Learning Projects
    • Organization: Agendas/Planners, Binder, Tiime Management, Goal Setting, STAR Strategy, Cornell Notes
    • Reading: Critical Reading Strategies, Note-Taking, Graphic Organizer, Summarizing, Recipirocal Teaching, Vocabulary Building, KWL
